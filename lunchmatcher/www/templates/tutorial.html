LunchMatcher uses the official LinkedIn API for login and access to the app to ensure the accuracy of the information provided by each user. Simply log in with your LinkedIn email and password. For the security of our users, the login information is only visible by LinkedIn. LunchMatcher will never gain access to your password.read more»

If matches are found, you will start receiving them every 15 minutes. You will see the other person’s photo, name, job title and company name as per LinkedIn, and you’ll need to decide whether to accept the match or not within the time limit. Keep in mind that rejecting or missing a match costs points!.read more»
After both parties accept, you’ll get a confirmation screen showing the exact venue and time slot for your lunch meeting, as well as address and contact info. Simply proceed to attend the lunch, and afterwards you’ll get a chance to rate your experience with both the person you met and the venue.read more»
